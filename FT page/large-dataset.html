Why Performance Matters

Nobody Likes Waiting for Slow Grids

When you're working with lots of data, slow loading and laggy scrolling can frustrate your users. They expect everything to work instantly—whether they're viewing 100 rows or 100,000 rows. A slow grid means lost productivity and unhappy users.

Our React Grid gives you several smart ways to keep things fast. Whether your users prefer scrolling continuously, jumping between pages, or loading data as they go, everything stays smooth and responsive. You pick what works best for your situation.

Lightning-fast loading Smooth, lag-free scrolling Works with huge datasets Instant response to clicks Happy, productive users

Pick the Best Way to Display Your Data

Smart Scrolling with Buffer

Only shows the rows you're currently looking at, plus a few extra above and below. As you scroll, it smoothly loads more. You can adjust how many extra rows to keep ready for the smoothest experience.

Best for: When you want super smooth scrolling and don't mind fine-tuning a few settings.

Choose this when you need the absolute smoothest scrolling experience and want control over performance.

Virtual Scrolling

Shows a fixed number of rows at a time based on your page size setting. As you scroll, it replaces what's shown with new rows. This keeps performance consistent and predictable no matter how much data you have.

Best for: When you want steady, reliable performance with a set number of rows always visible.

Choose this for a simple setup that delivers consistent scrolling performance every time.

Page-by-Page Navigation

Breaks your data into pages with buttons to jump between them—like Page 1, Page 2, Page 3. Users can easily skip to any page they want. Only one page loads at a time, keeping everything fast.

Best for: When users work through data in organized chunks or need to jump to specific pages.

Choose this for the most familiar experience—everyone knows how page numbers work.

Infinite Scrolling

Automatically loads more data as you scroll down—just like your social media feed. Users just keep scrolling and more rows appear seamlessly. No buttons to click, no waiting.

Best for: Modern apps where users want to browse continuously without interruption.

Choose this for a smooth, modern feel where users can explore data endlessly.

Common Questions

Can I use this with data from my server?
Yes! All these options work great with server data. The grid only fetches what it needs when it needs it, so your pages load faster and use less bandwidth.
Why are these options faster than regular grids?
Instead of loading everything at once, these smart options only show what's currently on your screen. This means less waiting, smoother scrolling, and better performance even with massive amounts of data.
Can I still filter and sort my data?
Absolutely! You can filter, sort, group, and use all the regular grid features. Everything still works great and stays fast no matter which option you choose.
Which option should I choose?
It depends on what feels natural for your users. Try paging if they work in organized chunks, infinite scrolling for casual browsing, or virtual scrolling for smooth continuous navigation. You can't go wrong—they're all fast!
Can I let users switch between different views?
Yes! You can let users toggle between these options on the fly. Some might prefer pages while others like continuous scrolling—you can give them the choice.
